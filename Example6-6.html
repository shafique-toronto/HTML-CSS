Flexbox Properties Explained:

  • display: flex - Enables flexbox for the container
  • flex-direction: row - Arranges items horizontally
  • justify-content: space-between - Distributes space between items
  • align-items: center - Vertically centers items
  • flex-wrap: wrap - Allows items to wrap to new lines
  • gap: 15px - Adds space between items
  • flex: 1 - Items grow equally to fill space
  • flex: 2 - Item 2 takes twice as much space as others
